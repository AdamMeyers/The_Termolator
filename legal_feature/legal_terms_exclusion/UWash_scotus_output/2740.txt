PER CURIAM.
The petition for rehearing is granted and the order denying the petition for writ of certiorari, , is set aside. The petition for a writ of certiorari is granted. The judgment of the Court of Appeals for the Second Circuit is vacated and the case is remanded to that court for further consideration in light of Bruton v. United States, . See Roberts v. Russell, ante, p. 293.
